To enable binary log in Mysql, edit the file:

<code>
/etc/mysql/my.cnf
</code>

Uncomment the log_bin line:

<code>
log_bin = /var/log/mysql/mysql-bin.log
</code>

<code>
expire_logs_days = 10
</code>

<code>
max_binlog_size = 100M
</code>
